Professional Nail Art & Care

Master the art of professional nail design and care with Manzoor Khan Academy's specialized nail training programs. Learn modern nail techniques including gel, acrylics, and nail art design that complements professional makeup and hairstyling services. Our expert instructors teach industry standards used in top salons and beauty studios.

From basic nail care to advanced nail art design, this program covers everything you need to provide comprehensive nail services. Learn nail health and safety protocols, product selection, design techniques, and client communication. Build your skills in one of the fastest-growing segments of the beauty industry.
